Why I Love the Coaster

By: Sarina Reuschle

October 18, 2017

On the majority of days, I take the Coaster from Carlsbad to Old Town. This is very convenient for a lot of different reasons such as:

  • generally on time departure and arrival
  • no traffic
  • free wifi (although not reliable)

While those things are all great, here are the top 3 reasons why YOU should consider taking the Coaster too

  1. people watching
  2. friendly conductors
  3. fantastic views

So, next time you need to get from North County to South or vice versa, hop on the Coaster!
